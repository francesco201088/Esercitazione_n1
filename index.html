Focaccia with caramelized onions, cranberries and Brie

Wow your tasta buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas
Part of FEATURED IN:
Fresh cranberries

Ingredienti

Per la focaccia

  • 4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups lukewarm water
  • 1/4 extra vergin oil, plus more for pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie cut into cube
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-size mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
